How Braid Works

Create threads of data from other services

Braid works by pulling data from your favourite services and then keeps a reference of all that data. Once it’s in our system, you’re given the ability to manage and modify the data as you please. This means you could provide the functionality and features you wish these services already had the ability to do.

Modify content using Modifiers

Once you’ve pulled in the data from a service, known as a thread, you’ll be able to attach modifiers giving you new ways of using your data. Want to be able to pull in videos from YouTube and list them by your own categories? You’ll be able to do that in Braid.

Expose your data from a braid using a simple API

With the content now managed in one place and modified until your hearts content. The threads of data are weaved into a braid where it is exposed through one unified REST API for your user. Where and how you use it is up to you.

Managed through a simple admin dashboard

When you sign up for braid you are presented with a simple admin area. With a clear focus on seeing what services you are pulling in (threads) managing and modifying your data is as simple as ever.
